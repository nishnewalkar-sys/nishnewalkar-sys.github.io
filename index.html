Frequently Asked Questions

What is the confidentiality policy?

All sessions are completely confidential and held with strict privacy standards, except where legally required to disclose (harm to self/others).

How long are counselling sessions?

Standard sessions are typically 50-60 minutes. The duration can be adjusted based on individual needs and requirements.

What age groups do you work with?

I specialize in adolescent counselling (teens and young adults) and also work with adults in various capacities including forensic consultancy.

Do you offer online counselling?

Please contact me directly to discuss available options and arrangements for your specific needs.

How do I book an appointment?

You can contact me through the contact form below or reach out via phone/email. Initial consultations are available to discuss your needs.

What credentials do you hold?

I hold a B.A. in Economics, Diploma in Counselling Psychology, and Diploma in Forensic & Adolescent Psychology, registered with EMO Matrix.
